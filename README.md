# Ban do Khang chien 1946 - 1950

Web tuong tac cho mon **Lich su Dang** voi chu de:
**Duong loi khang chien toan quoc va qua trinh to chuc thuc hien (1946 - 1950)**.

## Y tuong chinh

Nguoi hoc kham pha cac diem nong tren "ban do khang chien" (Ha Noi, Hai Phong, Viet Bac, Lang Son, Cao Bang, Sai Gon, Tra Vinh),
mo tung su kien, thuc hien mini-quiz, danh dau nhiem vu va mo khoa "bi mat lich su".

## Tinh nang

- Ban do tuong tac co trang thai khoa/mo theo tien trinh.
- Tien do kham pha + tien do hoan thanh theo phan tram.
- Huy hieu gamification theo cac moc hoc tap.
- Mini-quiz moi dia diem, giai thich dap an ngay tai cho.
- Kho "bi mat lich su" mo dan theo tung dia diem da chinh phuc.
- Truc thoi gian 1946 - 1950 de tong hop duong loi va dien bien.
